# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines (Glaido)

When generating text or copywriting for Glaido, you **MUST** strictly adhere to this brand persona.

## Brand Personality & Audience
* **Audience:** Tech-savvy individuals looking for extremely fast, efficient, and precise dictation solutions.
* **Tone:** Modern, high-energy, direct, highly professional, but approachable.
* **Keywords:** Swift, precise, frictionless, elite, high-performance.

## Grammar & Mechanics Rules
* **Headings:** Use **Title Case** for H1 and H2 (e.g., "Dictate Your Ideas"). Use **sentence case** for H3 and lower (e.g., "Customize your microphone configuration").
* **Punctuation:** Strictly avoid exclamation points (!) in standard UI copy. We convey high-energy and confidence through strong active verbs, not punctuation.
* **Clarity:** Prefer short, concise, and punchy sentences. Use active voice ("Dictate instantly" instead of "Dictation can be started instantly").

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "Utilize" | "Use" | Keep copy simple and direct. |
| "In order to..." | "To..." | Avoid unnecessary words. |
| "Speech-to-text" | "Dictation" / "Instant Capture" | Sounds more premium, high-performance, and elegant. |
| "Users" | "Creators" / "Engineers" | Flatters the technical focus of our target demographic. |
